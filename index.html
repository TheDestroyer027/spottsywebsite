Spottsy

Coming Soon To A Server Near You

In development right now, as we speak, is a brand-new, clean, refreshed, and amazing experience for Discord music bots! It's one-of-a-kind, like no other.

What To Expect

You may be wondering, what can I expect? What cool features will I see? What makes Spottsy different from the rest? Will it stop my coffee from getting cold? Well, let's show you!

Embeds You Can Understand

Spottsy will feature embeds that make sense, displaying useful information without emojis or GIFs that make it look unappealing. :/

An App That Clears The Clutter

Spottsy will feature an app that does it all: bot settings, queuing, playlist management, and more!

It's Just Music

Unlike some other bots that play audio from video, Spottsy plays audio from audio sources only. This ensures consistent audio quality for songs, without long pauses for unnecessary conversations.

Collaborative Playlist

Spottsy will allow you and your friends to create a playlist where everyone can contribute, ensuring everyone's happiness. :D

And Some More

All of the above, and more features, will be added during development!
